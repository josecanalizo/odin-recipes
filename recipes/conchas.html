Conchas (Mexican Sweet Bread)

Description:

This Mexican rice recipe makes a wonderful restaurant-style side dish for any Mexican dinner. This fluffy Mexican rice recipe, seasoned with garlic salt and cumin, will quickly become a go-to side dish in your household.

Satisfy your sweet tooth with this irresistibly soft conchas recipe. Conchas are traditional Mexican sweet bread rolls (a.k.a. pan dulce). Its name comes from the roll’s seashell-like appearance. Conchas consist of a sweet bread base and crunchy sugar topping.

Prep time: 20 mins Cook time: 20 mins Additional Time: 1 hr 45mins total time: 2 hrs 25 mins servings: 12 Yield: 12 Conchas

Ingredients for the Bread:

  • Warm Water
  • Yeast
  • All-purpose flour
  • Evaporated Milk
  • White Sugar
  • Melted butter
  • An egg
  • Salt
  • Ground cinnamon

Ingredients for the Topping:

  • White Sugar
  • Softened Butter
  • all-purpose flour
  • Ground cinnamon
  • Vanilla extract

Steps:

  1. Activate the yeast
  2. Make the dough, then turn it out on a floured surface and knead until it’s elastic.
  3. Make the topping. Flavor half of the topping with cinnamon and the other half with vanilla.
  4. Roll the dough into balls and pat the topping onto each one.
  5. Cut grooves into the topping, then let rise again.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until lightly golden brown.

Conchas are a perfect dessert after eating a meal such as Enchiladas or Mexican rice!
